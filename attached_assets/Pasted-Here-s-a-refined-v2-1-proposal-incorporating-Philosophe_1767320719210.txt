Here's a refined v2.1 proposal, incorporating Philosopher more deeply for meta & context while streamlining for quicker workflows. I reduced to a core 7-step flow within 3 passes (7 AI calls total), made Philosopher trigger-based, and added opportunity identification. This can be coded in your workflow.ts as an extension of multiStepReview.
Optimized 3-Pass Structure
Pass 1: Foundation & Initial Findings (3 calls)

Philosopher sets meta-context (goals, opportunities).
ARCH generates findings/plan.
MECH validates + adds own findings.
Joint Resolution: Auto-merge simple conflicts; escalate to Philosopher if needed.

Pass 2: Refinement & Cross-Validation (2 calls)

ARCH updates plan from MECH feedback.
MECH validates updates + edge cases.
Philosopher checks alignment + spots adjacents (triggered if changes > threshold).

Pass 3: Final Resolution & Sign-off (2 calls)

ARCH finalizes roadmap.
MECH + Philosopher joint validation (combined call for efficiency).
